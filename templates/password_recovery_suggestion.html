How To Recover Your Password?

1. Try To enter your password in a prompt!

2. Tap Recover password

3. Enter your email address and tap next

4. An email will be sent to you, check the passcode copy it and paste it to the prompt and tap next.

5. When valid, you will be sent a temporary password to use to log in

6. Go to your profile and update your password with the password you can remember

7. Make sure you tap update and you will be all good to go!
